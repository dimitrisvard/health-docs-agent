# The Effects of Moderate vs. Aggressive Treatment With Valsartan + Amlodipine on Patients With Hypertension Uncontrolled by Angiotensin-Receptor Blocker (Herein, ARB) Monotherapy
_Source: https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT00666536 · Kind: trial_

## Summary
The purpose of this trial is to compare blood pressure lowering efficacy of moderate Valsartan + Amlodipine treatment regimen (160 / 5 mg) with that of aggressive regimen (320 / 10 mg) in patients uncontrolled on ARB monotherapy, other than Valsartan. This was a multicenter, randomized, double-blind, parallel-design Phase 4 trial (acronym EXTRA), with an enrollment of 728 participants, sponsored by Novartis.

## Conditions
- Hypertension

Keywords: Hypertension, adults, Valsartan + Amlodipine, Angiotensin Receptor Blockers.

## Interventions
**Aggressive treatment regimen (5/320 mg to 10/320 mg) — Active Comparator:** Valsartan + Amlodipine, daily: 320 mg + 5 mg (2 weeks); Valsartan + Amlodipine, daily: 320 mg + 10 mg (2 weeks); Valsartan + Amlodipine and Hydrochlorothiazide, daily: 320 mg + 10 mg and 12.5 mg (4 weeks); then 320 mg + 10 mg and 12.5 mg or 25 mg (optional titration) (4 weeks). Drug: valsartan and amlodipine.

**Moderate treatment regimen (5/160 mg) — Active Comparator:** Valsartan + Amlodipine, daily dose: 160 mg + 5 mg (4 weeks); Valsartan + Amlodipine and Hydrochlorothiazide, daily: 160 mg + 5 mg and 12.5 mg (4 weeks); then 160 mg + 5 mg and 12.5 mg or 25 mg (optional titration) (4 weeks). Drug: valsartan and amlodipine.

## Eligibility Criteria
Inclusion Criteria:

* Male and female outpatients
* 18 Years of age or older
* Mean Sitting Systolic Blood Pressure (the top number) greater than or equal to 150 mmHg and lower than 200 mmHg while on Angiotensin-Receptor Blocker monotherapy for a minimum period of 28 days prior to randomization

Exclusion Criteria:

* Mean Sitting Systolic Blood Pressure (the top number) greater than or equal to 200 mmHg and/or Mean Sitting Diastolic Blood Pressure greater than or equal to 120 mmHg
* Transient ischemic attack (mini-stroke), myocardial infarction (heart attack), all types of revascularization procedures in the last 6 months
* Treatment with valsartan or any combination antihypertensive treatment with 28 days prior to screening (Visit 1)

## Primary Outcomes
**Change From Baseline to Week 4 in Mean Sitting Systolic Blood Pressure (MSSBP)** (Baseline and Week 4).
